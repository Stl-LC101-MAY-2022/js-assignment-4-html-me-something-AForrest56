Been there. Done that. Can we go back?

In November of 2020 one of my sisters had planned to compete in the Disney Wine and Dine Half Marathon Weekend. She had planned to take a weekend trip to Florida to compete in the race. She already had the hotel figured out, the tickets for the race purchased and was just waiting to get the plane tickets.
Enter Covid-19!
Disney unfortunately cancelled the races for the health and safety of all people involved. My sister was left with a huge decision to make. Cancel a weekend Disney trip? That is not how this family rolls. Instead her and our mother got on the phone with the Disney travel employees to come to an agreement.
When they called to ask if they could still visit, they were told about the hotel being shut down during that time. Since less people would be traveling due to the virus risk, Disney had plans to refurbish some parts of their property (a.k.a. the hotel!). Disney does there best to help with guest experiences and they did not disappoint. They offered my mother and sister an upgraded hotel for the same dates and the same price! They also let them add 2 days to the stay as well as add a few more people. WOOHOO! Disney Family Vacation!
Our travel plans were set! Mom, 4 daughters and 1 grand-daughter are going to Disney! From November 5th to the 9th we would all stay at Disney's Animal Kingdom Villas-Kidani Village.
